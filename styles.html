Architectural Styles

Discover the defining styles that shaped the world of architecture.

Gothic Architecture

Gothic architecture is characterized by pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and flying buttresses. Famous examples include Notre Dame Cathedral and Chartres Cathedral.

Read More

Baroque Architecture

Baroque architecture is known for its grandeur, dramatic use of light and shadow, and elaborate decorations. Key examples are St. Peter's Basilica and the Palace of Versailles.

Read More

Modern Architecture

Modern architecture emphasizes functionality and simplicity, using materials like steel and glass. Examples include the Sydney Opera House and the Guggenheim Museum.

History of Architecture

Explore the fascinating evolution of architectural styles and techniques through history.

Ancient Architecture

Ancient architecture includes iconic structures such as the Pyramids of Egypt, the Parthenon in Greece, and the Colosseum in Rome. These designs reflect the early advancements in engineering and the cultural values of ancient civilizations.

Medieval Architecture

Gothic cathedrals and Romanesque churches characterized the Medieval period. Key features included pointed arches, flying buttresses, and large stained-glass windows, demonstrating the era's emphasis on spirituality and light.

Read More

Renaissance Architecture

The Renaissance marked a revival of classical styles, emphasizing symmetry, proportion, and geometry. Famous structures include St. Peter's Basilica in Rome and the Florence Cathedral.

Read More

Modern Architecture

Modern architecture is characterized by minimalist designs and innovative materials such as steel and glass. Iconic examples include skyscrapers and structures like the Sydney Opera House.
